About

I am a second-year Master student in VIPLAB at Department of Automation, Tsinghua University, where I am supervised by Prof. Feng Chen. I received my Bachelor's degree from Department of Automation, Tsinghua University in 2020.

My research interests are in training real-world intelligent robots and building AGI systems. Currently I am working on imitation learning, continual learning, domain generalization, and skill organization.

I am looking for a PhD position! Feel free to contact to me through E-mail or WeChat (ID: Chong-kai).
